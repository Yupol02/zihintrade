ZihinTrade Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Data Collection

ZihinTrade collects the following information:

  • Email Address: Used for user authentication and account management
  • Name: First name and last name for personalization
  • Trading Data: All trading transactions, strategies, emotions, and notes are stored locally on your device

2. Data Usage

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Personalizing your app experience
  • Storing your trading logs and analysis locally on your device
  • Providing you with trading performance reports and insights

3. Data Storage

Local Storage: All trading data (transactions, strategies, emotions, notes) is stored locally on your device using Room Database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Cloud Storage: Only your email address and name are stored securely using Firebase Authentication for login purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your trading data remains private and stored only on your device.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection (by not using the app)

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your data in the following ways:

  1. Through the App:
    • Open ZihinTrade app
    • Go to Profile screen
    • Click "Logout" button
    • All local data will be cleared from your device
  2. Contact Us: Email us at zihintrade@example.com to request account deletion

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User account information (email, name)
  • All trading transactions
  • Personal preferences and settings
  • All locally stored data on your device

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure login
  • Local encryption for sensitive data
  • No transmission of trading data to external servers

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:

Email: zihintrade@example.com

Subject: Privacy Policy Inquiry / Data Deletion Request
