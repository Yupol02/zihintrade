ZihinTrade - User Data Deletion

Last Updated: January 2025

At ZihinTrade, we respect your privacy and provide you with full control over your personal data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time using the methods described below.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User Account Information: Email address, first name, last name
  • Trading Transactions: All your trading logs, entry/exit prices, amounts
  • Strategies: All saved trading strategies and goals
  • Emotional Data: All emotion tracking data (before, during, after trade emotions)
  • Notes: All personal notes and comments
  • Preferences: App settings, theme preferences, language settings
  • Performance Data: All reports, statistics, and analysis data

How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Delete Data Through the App

Step 1: Open the ZihinTrade app on your device
Step 2: Navigate to the "Profile" screen (bottom navigation bar)
Step 3: Scroll down and click the "Logout" button
Step 4: Confirm the logout action

Result: All local data stored on your device will be immediately cleared. Your Firebase Authentication account will also be logged out.

Method 2: Request Account Deletion via Email

If you prefer to request account deletion via email, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Send an email to: yusufpolat0109@gmail.com
Step 2: Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - ZihinTrade"
Step 3: Include the following information in your email:
  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name (as registered in the app)
  • Confirmation that you want to delete your account and all associated data
Step 4: We will process your request within 7 business days

Result: Once processed, your Firebase Authentication account will be deleted, and all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems.

Important Notes

  • Data Storage: Most of your trading data is stored locally on your device. When you delete the app or clear app data, this information is immediately removed.
  • Cloud Data: Your email and authentication data stored in Firebase will be deleted upon account deletion request.
  • Permanent Deletion: Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please ensure you have backed up any important information before deletion.
  • Third-Party Services: If you logged in using Facebook or Google, you may need to revoke app permissions from those platforms separately.

Revoking Third-Party Permissions

If you logged in using Facebook or Google, you should also revoke app permissions:

  • Facebook: Go to Facebook Settings > Apps and Websites > Remove ZihinTrade
  • Google: Go to Google Account > Security > Third-party apps with account access > Remove ZihinTrade
